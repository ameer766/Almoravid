ABOUT US

We are talented at bringing projects to life.

Real Estate & Development:

Engages in real estate ownership, development, and management. This includes acquiring land and buildings, property development, and offering general contracting services for construction, maintenance, and alteration of structures.

Contracting & Construction:

Provides comprehensive construction services including building railways, tramways, roads, houses, pipelines, docks, bridges, and other infrastructure projects. Additionally, offers maintenance and alteration services.

Transportation & Logistics:

Specializes in land-based transportation services, including haulage and courier services. Operates garages and offers vehicle ownership and chartering services for various types of motor vehicles.

Agriculture & Farming:

Involved in farming activities such as poultry, fisheries, fruit farming, and agro-allied services. Manufactures and sells livestock feed, liquid fertilizers, pesticides, and farm implements.

Retail & Distribution:

Operates supermarkets, departmental stores, and other retail outlets for wholesale and retail sales. Involved in the manufacturing and distribution of various goods, including general merchandise and building materials.

Trade & Merchandise:

Engages in buying, selling, importing, and exporting a wide range of goods. Acts as general merchants and industrialists, dealing in products such as beverages, building materials, and other general goods.
